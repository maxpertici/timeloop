# Timeloop

**Time tracking, simplified.**

Timeloop is a minimalist time tracking application designed for people who want to log their activities without being distracted by unnecessary features.

## Philosophy

### Minimalist
Timeloop takes a clean approach to time tracking. No cluttered dashboards, no dozens of configuration options. The interface focuses on what matters: **creating entries, logging time, and understanding where your time goes**.

### Productive
The goal is simple: **log your time efficiently and calculate totals quickly**. No complex workflows, no endless clicks. Just fast time entry and instant calculations when you need them.

## How it works

### Track View - Log your time
1. Search or create an entry (e.g., "Client meeting", "Design work")
2. Optional: Assign a category
3. Click the entry to open details
4. Add time using your preferred method:
   - Minutes: `90` (90 minutes)
   - Hours: `1.5` (1 hour 30 minutes)
   - Time range: `08:00 â†’ 12:00` (4 hours)
5. Optional: Add a note, select a date
6. Done! Your time is logged

### Count View - Calculate totals
1. Select a period (today, this week, this month, last 30 days, or custom)
2. Select one or more entries
3. See the total time calculated automatically

### Entries View - Manage your data
Browse all your entries, filter by period, search, assign categories in batch, or delete entries.
